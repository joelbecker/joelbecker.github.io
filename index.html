Hi. I'm Joel Becker.


I'm an early career data scientist and a recently graduated Bachelor of Knowledge Integration. I currently work as a software developer for the University of Waterloo's NetLab, creating data integration and data extraction tools which enable our interdisciplinary research team's leading-edge research in large-scale collaboration networks. My own research has spanned from social network research analyzing large-scale trends in the intellectual diversity of social science research teams, to organizational research studying the impact of workplace supervisors' race and gender on their subordinates' reactions to fair or unfair treatment, to topic modelling online responses to the syrian refugee crisis.
